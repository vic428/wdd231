Project Website Plan

Site Name: Access Point Foundation

Access Point is a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) dedicated to supporting deserving BYU-Pathway students by providing laptops, tuition fee assistance, data subscription support and tutorial services in real time. The term “Access Point” literally means a place where connection happens — which fits my mission perfectly: helping students gain access to education, funding, and opportunities.

optional domain: theaccesspoint.org

Site Purpose

The website will serve as the official digital platform of the NGO, showcasing its mission, initiatives, and impact. It will provide details about ongoing programs such as laptop donations, tuition sponsorships, and data subscription aid. Visitors will be able to learn how to apply for assistance, view success stories, and access resources that support their educational journey. Additionally, the site will include features for donors and partners to contribute financially or in kind, as well as a contact page with the NGO’s details and social media links.

Scenarios

The following are common questions or scenarios site visitors representing our target audience may ask:

  • How do i apply for a data/Internet subscription scholarship?
  • Where do i find information on eligibility criterias for any of the available scholarship offerings?
  • When would applications be open for tuition fee scholarship applications?
  • How do i donate and support Access Points Effort in helping BYU-Pathway Students in Africa?
